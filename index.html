Live Shows

I perform lots of live shows in Kolkata and beyond, including Nepal, Bhutan, Birganj (Bihar), Jadavpur (Kolkata), Habra, and Barasat. My versatile repertoire spans Bollywood hits, Bengali classics, romantic ballads, Sufi melodies, and classical music, captivating audiences with every performance. With over seven years of experience, I bring passion and energy to each stage, making every event unforgettable.

Cafe Shows

I perform in many cafes in Kolkata, including prestigious 5-star and 7-star venues. Some of the cafes where I perform are AltAir, Sabka Club, Red Express Cafe, Barbeque Nation, Absolute Barbecues CC2, Park Street, CC1, and many more. My versatile repertoire spans Bollywood hits, Bengali classics, romantic ballads, Sufi melodies, and classical music, creating an enchanting ambiance for every audience.

Big Events

I perform at major events like Jadavpur Sanskriti, Win Chatro Youva, Ashokenagar Festival, Holi events in Barasat, and many more.
